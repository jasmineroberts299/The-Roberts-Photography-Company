Welcome to our History page.

We were founded in 1815, when The Robert family consisted of painters and sculpters. Bill Robert, who was the head of the family, thought that it would be a great idea to create a machine that captured photographic stills of moments instead of having to use paint and clay to reenact those moments. He called it "The Picture Thingy". It was a big machine that stood on 3 legs and you had to put your head under a hood and hold a light with one arm. This was the original camera before the patented camera was made in 1816.

Since then, each generation of the Robert family have been going to school for photography and continuing the legacy.
